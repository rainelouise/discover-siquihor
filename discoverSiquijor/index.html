Welcome to Siquijor

It's time to start your adventure

Nestled in the heart of the Visayas, Siquijor is a paradise of pristine beaches, mystical waterfalls, and breathtaking landscapes. Known as the "Island of Fire," it captivates visitors with its enchanting sunsets, rich culture, and warm hospitality.

Explore hidden lagoons, take a refreshing dip in Cambugahay Falls, or walk along the powdery white sands of Salagdoong Beach. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a touch of magic, Siquijor promises an unforgettable experience.
